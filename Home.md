### Pipeline Stages 
Pipeline stages can be named. If the "name" field is omitted, stages are named in sequence: s1, s2, etc. Stage operation is defined by the "op" field:
* [[op: imread]]
* [[op: imwrite]]
* [[op: HoleRecognizer]]
